FAQ

Is my data sent to your servers?

No. Everything runs in your browser. Your conversations never leave your device. Lore uses your own API key to process conversations.

Which AI services are supported?

Claude, ChatGPT, and Gemini. Any conversation you can copy, Lore can structure. The Chrome extension also supports one-click capture from all three.

What happens after the free tier?

Free gives you 20 transforms per day and 3 projects. Pro unlocks unlimited everything for $12/mo ($10/mo annual).

Can I export my data?

Yes. Export to Markdown, JSON, or copy to clipboard. No data lock-in, ever.

Is it open source?

Yes. Fully open source on GitHub. You can inspect the code, contribute, or self-host.
